About Us

Established in 2018 in Roorkee, Uttarakhand, Bhuamrit is a farmer producer company registered under the Company Act 2013 holding more than 500 farmers in our organization.The farmers belong to the Bhagwanpur area, which is situated at Khubaanpur, Uttarakhand and know to supplying organic products to retail markets. Derived from two words, Bhumi means organic and Amrit means good quality. While growing our produce, we do not use any chemicals or pesticides on our plants and other products and adhere to environmental standards at all times.

Our aim is to provide chemical-free food and to build a healthy nation by offering good nutrition and promoting a sustainable environment. By working together, we support each other and bridge the gap between farmers and the market. We have a wide range of products and are continuously adding more to our list.

While we follow agricultural practices passed down from our ancestors, we also incorporate the latest agricultural technologies to maximize productivity from the land.

Organic farming avoids the use of synthetic fertilizers, pesticides, growth regulators, and chemically treated livestock feed. Our products are always organic, hygienic, and quality assured.
